The Ultimate Greece Trip

Welcome to Greece, a country with a rich history, vibrant culture, and stunning landscapes. Our itinerary will take you on a journey through some of the most popular destinations in Greece, as well as some hidden gems that are off the beaten path

This strategically located stay in the heart of Athens is only a stone's throw away from Syntagma square and Ermou street, offering easy access to numerous cultural, shopping, and dining options. Guests can enjoy fully equipped, stylish rooms and suites with WiFi access and Smart LED TVs, as well as sound proofing and air-conditioning. The hotel is a blend of timeless modernity, featuring architectural, design, and artistic elements by famous Greek artists. Some rooms offer stunning views of the Acropolis from either the rooms or the rooftop garden.

EXPERIENCE

Highlights of Athens Tour

Discover the highlights of Athens, along with the city’s best kept secrets, on a private 5-hour tour. The half-day adventure is perfect for travelers who like the flexibility to customize their sightseeing experience without worrying about the details. Visit the Temple of Zeus, Roman Agora, Acropolis, and much more in private luxury transportation. Along the way, your guide provides immersive commentary and insider tips for the rest of your Athens vacation.

TASTE

Eat dinner in a revolving restaurant suspended in the sky during this thrilling experience in Athens. From a bird's eye view at 165 feet in the air, get views of the city of Athens lit up at night, including the illuminated Acropolis, as the table slowly rotates. With 22 dining companions, feast on a 6-course meal of modern and traditional Greek dishes with unlimited wine, beer, and soft drinks.

EXPERIENCE

Our first destination on this adventure is nestled on the slopes of the Caldera, this picturesque spot boasts unparalleled serenity and beauty. Here, you'll find yourself immersed in the essence of Santorini - from the majestic skies blending seamlessly into the never-ending sea, to the charming Cycladic homes lining quaint footpaths. It's truly a feast for the senses! Next up perched high above the island, breathtaking vistas will leave you in awe. And let's not forget about the world-famous Church, which stands tall as a beacon of Santorini's rich culture and history. You won't want to miss this must-see landmark on your journey through the island!

EXPERIENCE

Greek tavern

After soaking up the magic of the beach, you will enjoy delicious Greek cuisine at a traditional Greek tavern. Food is served on a large platter and there are two options available: Meat Platter and Fish Platter. Each platter serves two people and therefore diners should state their preference in pairs.

VIBE

Cruise around Santorini on this half-day catamaran tour departing from Vlihada Marina. Observe famed sights such as the Venetian Lighthouse, the Indian Rocks, the Red Beach and the White Beach as you glide atop clear blue water. Enjoy a swimming and snorkeling excursion, then take in panoramic views of the Aegean Sea and the small neighboring Cyclades Islands during the return trip. During the trip indulge in a buffet-style BBQ meal and sip drinks from the open bar on board.

I enjoyed a half-day catamaran tour from Vlihada Marina around Santorini. I saw the Venetian Lighthouse, the Indian Rocks, the Red Beach, and the White Beach. I also swam, snorkeled, and took in the panoramic views of the Aegean Sea and Cyclades Islands. I indulged in a buffet-style BBQ meal and drinks from the open bar on board. It was an unforgettable experience.
